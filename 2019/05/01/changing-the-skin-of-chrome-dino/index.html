    Tutorial on installing texture packs of Chrome Dino

    Step 1

    Open Chrome Dino anywhere. from chrome://dino maybe

    Step 2

    Press F12, turn to Console and input: Runner.imageSprite.src="<Your image path>" with quotes Note that your image path should be on the INTERNET.

    Step 3

    Done!
