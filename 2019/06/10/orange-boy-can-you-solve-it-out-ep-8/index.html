    Input {[1,5],w=3} {[2,4],w=5} {[3,5],w=1} Output Explain choose 1 -> [1,5] choose 2 -> [2,4] choose 3 -> [3,5] choose 1,2 -> [2,4] choose 1,3 -> [3,5] choose 2,3 -> [3,4] choose 1,2,3 -> [3,4]
