Hi, I am ZJS! Your lovely♂friend~ I am from TengFei country. A country far to the East. In this series, I will lead you into the beauty of Tengfei.

Introduction

Name

  • English: Tengfei
  • Chinese: 腾飞国

Flag

  • Flag:

Song

  • Anthem: 跑步(Running)

Range

  • The range of tengfei is shown in red

City

  • Captial:客厅(Living Room)
  • Largest City:主席卧室(President Bedroom)

Language

  • Offical Language: Chinese, Tengfeish
  • Recognised regional languages: English(HHS)
  • Offical Script: Chinese
  • Ethnics Groups: 100% of TF-Hans
  • Demonym:Chinese

Government

  • President: 赵金松(Jinsong Zhao)
  • Wife of president: 俞萍(Ping Yu)

Legislature

  • Any of the 炒鸡跳绳者(Fried-Chicken Rope-Jumping Man)

Formation

  • Created at 2017

Area

  • Total: about 0.0001 km2
  • Water: 0%

Population

  • 3 in total
  • density 30000/km2

Currency

  • 腾飞币(TFB)
  • 1 TFB = 1 Bitcoin always.

Other

  • Time zone:UTC+8 (China Standard Time)
  • Date format yyyy-mm-dd or yyyy年m月d日 (CE; CE-1949)
  • Driving side right
  • Calling code +114
  • ISO 3166 code JS
  • Internet TLD .js .腾飞 .zjs
